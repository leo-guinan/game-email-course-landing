Find Your Game

A 5-day email course to identify which creator game you're playing—and why your content isn't compounding.

There are 6 creator games.

Most creators are playing 3-4 at once without knowing it.

That's why your content doesn't compound. Your audience can't pattern-match. Your leverage stays weak. Your systems break when you try to delegate.

This course helps you see it.

What you'll do:

Over 5 days, you'll run a series of prompts against your own content.

  • Day 1: Identify your primary game
  • Day 2: Audit your last 10 posts for consistency
  • Day 3: Diagnose your collision pattern
  • Day 4: Draft a piece of "clean" content
  • Day 5: Understand the limits of self-diagnosis

Each email takes 10-15 minutes. You'll need access to Claude or ChatGPT.

What you'll have at the end:

  • Your primary game (the one you're actually playing)
  • Your aspirational game (the one you think you're playing)
  • Your collision pattern (which games are conflicting)
  • Your consistency score (how often you drift)
  • One piece of clean content (so you feel the difference)

This is for you if:

  • You're posting consistently but nothing compounds
  • You've tried different formats/topics/schedules and you're still stuck
  • You suspect something structural is broken but can't name it

Not for you if:

  • You're just starting out (you need reps first, not diagnosis)
  • You're happy with your current trajectory
  • You want motivation (this is structural, not inspirational)
